Product Description

Vamos! The Babolat Vamos Rafa dampener is designed to reduce unwanted vibrations and shock on the tennis court. This pack includes two dampeners that fit any racquet. Pair them with a Babolat Pure Aero Rafa for a perfect cosmetic match and enhance your playing experience.

Product Features

  • Two Babolat Vamos Rafa Vibration Dampeners included
  • Reduces unwanted vibrations and shock
  • Fits any racquet

Product Purpose

  • A vibration dampener is intended to lessen the shock that is produced when the tennis ball strikes the racquet strings during contact. This primarily affects two features of the stringbed: its feel and sound. Because of the dampener, you experience less impact. This is ideal for beginning players because it lessens the annoying feel of mishits. Furthermore, the pinging sound that is audible is diminished to a pop or thud sound.
